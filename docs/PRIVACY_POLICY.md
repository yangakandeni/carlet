# Privacy Policy for Carlet

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Carlet ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Carlet" or the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

**Phone Number**
- Required for account creation and authentication
- Used to verify your identity via SMS one-time password (OTP)
- Stored securely in our database

**Name**
- Collected during onboarding
- Used to personalize your experience and identify you to other users (if you choose not to post anonymously)
- Optional: You may update or remove your name at any time

**Vehicle Information**
- Car make and model
- License plate number
- Collected during onboarding
- Used to match you with reports about your vehicle
- Helps notify you when someone reports an issue with your car

**Profile Photo** (Optional)
- You may upload a profile photo
- Displayed on your reports (unless posting anonymously)
- Can be deleted or changed at any time

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data**
- We collect your approximate location when you create a report
- Used to show nearby car alerts to relevant users
- Location precision: City/neighborhood level (not exact GPS coordinates)
- You can deny location permission, but this will limit app functionality

**Photos**
- When you create a report, you take or upload a photo of the car issue
- Photos are required to verify the legitimacy of reports
- Photos are stored in our secure cloud storage and associated with your report

**Device Information**
- Device type and model (e.g., iPhone 14, Samsung Galaxy S23)
- Operating system version (iOS/Android version)
- App version
- Device identifiers (for push notifications)
- Used for technical support and app improvement

**Usage Data**
- Reports you create
- Comments you post
- Reports you mark as resolved
- Interactions with the app (likes, reactions)
- Used to improve app functionality and user experience

### 3. Information From Third Parties

**Google Maps**
- We use Google Maps to display location data
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Firebase (Google)**
- We use Firebase for authentication, database, storage, and push notifications
- Firebase's privacy policy applies: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Authenticate and verify your account
   - Create and display car alert reports
   - Match reports to vehicle owners
   - Send push notifications about relevant alerts
   - Enable communication through comments

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Personalize your feed with nearby alerts
   - Remember your preferences
   - Provide customer support

3. **Safety and Security**
   - Verify the legitimacy of reports (photo requirement)
   - Prevent spam and abuse
   - Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
   - Enforce our Terms of Service

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Respond to legal requests
   - Protect our rights and property

5. **Analytics and Improvement**
   - Understand how users interact with the app
   - Identify bugs and technical issues
   - Improve features and performance

## How We Share Your Information

We **DO NOT** sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### With Other Users
- **Public Information:** Reports you create (including photos, license plate, message, location) are visible to all app users
- **Anonymous Reports:** If you choose to post anonymously, your name and profile photo are hidden
- **Profile Information:** Your name and profile photo are visible to other users in the app
- **Comments:** Comments you post on reports are visible to all users

### With Service Providers
- **Firebase/Google Cloud:** For authentication, database, storage, and push notifications
- **Google Maps:** For location services and map display
- These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and cannot use your data for other purposes

### For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Protection of users' rights and safety

### Business Transfers
If Carlet is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days
- **Reports:** Reports you created may remain visible (anonymized after account deletion)
- **Legal Requirements:** We may retain certain data if required by law
- **Backup Systems:** Data may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days after deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update
- View and edit your profile information in the app
- Update your name, vehicle information, and profile photo
- Change your phone number (requires re-verification)

### Delete Your Data
- Delete your account and personal information by contacting us
- Reports you created will be anonymized (personal info removed)
- Photos in reports will remain to preserve report validity

### Location Permissions
- You can disable location permissions in your device settings
- This will limit your ability to create reports with location data

### Push Notifications
- You can disable push notifications in your device settings or app settings
- You will still receive important account-related notifications

### Anonymity
- Choose to post reports anonymously (hides your name and photo)
- Your phone number and vehicle info remain private regardless

### Data Portability
- Request a copy of your data by contacting us
- We will provide your data in a machine-readable format

Carlet is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Firebase Security:** Authentication and database access are secured with Firebase Security Rules
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access user data
- **Photo Verification:** Required photos help prevent spam and abuse
- **Regular Security Reviews:** We regularly review and update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
